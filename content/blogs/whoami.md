# Hello, I am <span class="name">**CodingBoyBlah**</span>.
Well, that's just my online username. My name's Anshuman. I live in India.

As apparent from my username, I like coding. I first started learning programming in 2021, when I learnt Python.

I kind of just stuck around in my Python comfort zone for a year, until I decided to check out game development.
I learnt GDScript and Godot, and pretty much made some shitty games for some time. 

In 2022, I released my first (and only) game on itch.io, called [Circle Pong](https://codingboyblah.itch.io/circle-pong). I showed it to many people on discord, and got positive feedback.

Honestly this is what made me realise that this coding thing might just work out for me.

In November of 2024, I learnt JavaScript. It opened up a whole new world for me. Thats the day I transitioned from an immature little python kid, to an immature little JavaScript enthusiast.

I made many projects in JS, such as vynl, and also this portfolio :D

Recently, I've also been getting into designing. I've made some posters (which I have posted on my twitter). I have been enjoying it a lot (although I'm still learning).

I'm not one to have great expectations about my future. I don't have it all mapped out yet, but I want to become a software developer, or designer, or something that pays the rent.

I love building meaningless projects in my free time. I just hope I can make a living out of it.  

This site is basically me putting my stuff out there instead of letting it rot on my laptop. I’ll post things I build, things I break (you're gonna see these more often), and whatever I’m learning along the way.

I don't have a big plan, but let's see where this goes.
